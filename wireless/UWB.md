Applications:
- distance measure (cm level accuracy) - measures travel time. Short ranges (<50m).
- high speeddata communications
- security
- low power consumption for battery based systems.

Overview:
- Low energy levels (around noise level)
- Freq: 3-11 GHz.
- BW: ~500 MHz.
- short pulses: ~2ns.
